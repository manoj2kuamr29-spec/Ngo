    NGO Darpan

    NGO Darpan is a platform managed by NITI Aayog, Government of India. Our registration on this portal ensures transparency and accountability.

    80G Certificate

    This certificate is issued under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. It allows donors to our organization to avail a tax deduction on their contributions, encouraging greater financial support for our charitable activities.

    Registration Certificate

    Our FCRA registration ensures we can legally receive funds from foreign sources and our reports are filed regularly to maintain compliance.

    12A Certificate

    The 12A registration is crucial for any non-profit organization. It grants us tax-exempt status, meaning our income is not subject to income tax.

    Bylaws Certificate

    Our organization's bylaws are a set of rules and regulations that govern our operations. This document outlines our purpose, membership, board structure, and meeting procedures, ensuring proper governance.

    PAN Card

    Our organization holds a Permanent Account Number (PAN) card, which is a unique 10-digit alphanumeric identifier issued by the Indian Income Tax Department. It is essential for all financial transactions and tax-related matters.

    GST Certificate

    While most non-profits are exempt, our GST certificate, if applicable, would signify our compliance with the Goods and Services Tax regulations for any taxable services we might provide.

    CSR Certificate

    This certificate relates to Corporate Social Responsibility and allows corporate entities to fulfill their CSR obligations by funding our projects. It's a key document for building partnerships with the corporate sector.
